About Shalomville InnoTech Academy

Preparing your future through quality education and innovative technology training

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Shalomville InnoTech Academy began as a small coding bootcamp with a simple mission: to make tech education accessible to everyone. What started as a single classroom with 10 students has grown into a premier technology training institution with thousands of graduates.

Our founders, a group of industry veterans, recognized the growing gap between traditional education and the skills needed in the modern workforce. They set out to create a learning environment that was practical, hands-on, and focused on real-world applications.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 3,000 students launch careers in technology. Our alumni work at top companies including Google, Amazon, Microsoft, and countless startups around the world.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality technology education that equips students with the skills and confidence to succeed in the digital economy.

Our Vision

A world where anyone, regardless of background, can access the education needed to thrive in technology careers.

Our Values

  • Excellence in education
  • Student-centered approach
  • Innovation in teaching
  • Community building
